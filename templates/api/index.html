About
URconnected is a company focused on connecting urban to rural with the motto of helping farmers. Facilitating the farmers in raising funds and helping them practice organic farming with expert advice and avoiding food wastages. Instilling confidence in farmers to have good farm and yield without worrying about the loss and users to have quality of food.

Services

URconnected has identified Four key services which are instrumental for success. One key is providing platform for raising funds for farmers. Second key is encouraging organic farming which is very important in present scenario. Third key is Knowledge transfer in farming across the country with effective solution in quick time. Fourth is Reduce Food wastage.
Funds

Connecting Urban to rural and helping farmers to raise funds without approaching bank for loans.

Organic Farming

The main aim is to unwrap the process of farming to the end user so that they will be having confidence in what they are eating. Orgaic farming the mst important thing for healthy India.

Knowledge Sharing

We as a company form a platform for knowledge building and sharing of farming practices, technical support and resolving crises in crop in no time.

India with a population of 1.2 billion in which many are not accessible for quality food. Wastage of food is a big problem which is more than 30% now. We try to reduce that to minimum level.
